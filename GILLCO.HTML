About Us

Gillco International School is one of the top educational schools in Mohali. An endeavour of The Gillco Group, our primary goal is to educate with passion and expand the horizons of our students at the same time. We strongly believe that every individual adds value and hence deserves respect. This ideology forms the core value of Gillco International School which has made it rank in the top 10 CBSE Schools in Mohali. Since inception, Gillco International School has set the benchmark in fostering all-round development of its students by catering not only to the cognitive domain, but also exposing the students to new vistas of physical, socio-cultural, emotional, and spiritual learning.Every child is unique in themselves and the environment Gillco functions in with its students, seeks to maximize their inherent potential.All classrooms have 25 to 30 students only which helps us maintain ‘The Teacher to Taught Ratio’ at a healthy 1:25. The fee has been decided after a thorough economic analysis of all segments of the society. Thus, making it one of the most affordable schools in Mohali. We, at Gillco International School, invite you to experience our model of education that is on par with the standards of a classic international school.

Admissions

Admission is subject to clearing the Entrance Test/Interaction. A clear calendar month’s notice for withdrawal from the school rolls must be submitted in writing by the parents, failing which one month’s fee / the fee for one quarter will be charged thereof. Written intimation of withdrawal will help us in being prompt with your refunds and your child’s transcripts and other documents. No allowances will be made for broken periods if withdrawal is to take effect within the last two months of the academic session or in the final term, in which case full fees for the term must be paid The concerned student’s T.C (Transfer Certificate) can be issued only after three weeks of the receipt of the withdrawal application. The School Leaving Certificate will not be issued unless all school dues are paid.

Academics

To inculcate good values in our students, the morning assembly is an important daily routine at Gillco International School. A specific house or a class, according to the daily duty roster, is assigned to lead the assembly. The four houses at Gillco are Orion, Polaris, Thalia, and Urania. With a keen focus on public speaking, value-based assemblies are conducted every day at Gillco International School. Extra-curricular activities like these help in shaping the overall personality of a student and help us maintain our status as one of the top CBSE schools in Kharar.
